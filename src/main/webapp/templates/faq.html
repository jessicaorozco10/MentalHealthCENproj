Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Find answers to common questions about our services.



1. What is this platform about?

This platform connects clients with therapists, offering online therapy sessions, mental health resources, and support tools.

2. Who can use this platform?

Individuals seeking mental health support and certified therapists offering services can use the platform.

3. How do I sign up?

Click "Register" on the homepage, fill in your details, and verify your email.

4. Is this platform available on mobile devices?

Yes, it is accessible via web browsers on mobile devices.

Account & Security

5. How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions.

6. What security measures are in place?

We use MFA, encryption, and HIPAA compliance for data protection.

Therapy & Appointments

7. How do I schedule an appointment?

Select a therapist, choose an available slot, and confirm your booking.

8. Can I reschedule or cancel an appointment?

Yes, you can modify your appointments through your dashboard.

Payments & Subscription

9. How do I pay for therapy sessions?

Payments can be made securely via credit/debit cards.

Mental Health Resources

10. What resources are available?

Access articles, exercises, mood tracking, meditation guides, and crisis resources.
